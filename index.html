    About Us


    DownTown Cleaning and Gardening Services is a well established cleaning and gardening company. Our intention is to establish ourselves as the leading company in this industry in South Africa. We aim to be recognized leader in providing accredited services to satisfy individual client's needs.


    DownTown Cleaning and Gardening Services was started by Christian Mhlope, who's intention was to start a company that would offer unique and tailored services to its clientele. The company's integrated cleaning and gardening model incorporates the manpower, technology and superior management strategy necessary to achieve this tailored services for its clientele.


    At DownTown Cleaning and Gardening Services, we are delivering effective cleaning and gardening services without compromising quality. We aspire to deliver a prime service to our clients, as required, with affordable competitive market prices


    We presently service tens of clients and thousands of square feet every day of the week. This varies from small once-a-week offices with just a few staff members to large clients with hundreds of staff members, we are stll providing an array of quality services no matter how big or small the challenge is.
